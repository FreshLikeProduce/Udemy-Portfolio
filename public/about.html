About Me

Stephanie Cwalinski (she/they) grew up in Rockland and Orange County, NY. She graduated from Stony Brook University in 2013 with a BA in Psychology. She went on to live in South Korea until late 2019, where she taught English as a Second Language.

Now, Stephanie has embraced coding as her next long-term pursuit. She believes, foremost, that technology must serve people such that they may be free to pursue their own interests and passions, and lead happier lives.
